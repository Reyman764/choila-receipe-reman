CHHWELA

Choila,sometimes Chhwela or Chhoyela (Nepali: छोयला) is a typical Newari dish that consists of spiced grilled buffalo meat.




INGREDIENTS AND QUANTITY

INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
Buff 250 gram
Cumin Seed 30 gram
Tomato 1 nos
Coriander leaf Few
Salt As Taste
Chilli Flakes 5 gram
Timur 5 gram
Ginger Garlic Taste 5 gram
Lemon Juice 5 ml
Roasted Mustard Oil 15 ml

METHOD OF PREPERATION

  • Chop the corainder leaf.
  • boil buff pieces with tomato together.
  • Skew the boiled buff and tomato into skewer and cook in open flame of gas for few minutes.
  • Roast the cumin seeds/jeera and blend it in a food blender.
  • Take a bowl, add blended roasted cumin powder, chilli flakes, garlic leaves, Sichuan pepper, Tomato, Salt, Buff and GG paste. Add roasted mustard oil and mix it properly with hand.
  • Garnish by adding corriander leaf.
